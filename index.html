Total Dose Calculator by BSA

Disclaimer: This calculator provides an estimate of the total dose needed based on the Body Surface Area (BSA), dose per m², frequency of treatment, and duration of treatment. The calculation is based on the following formula:

Total Dose = BSA × Dose per m² × Frequency × Duration

Please note that this calculator provides an estimate and should not replace medical advice. Always consult with a healthcare professional for accurate dosing recommendations.
